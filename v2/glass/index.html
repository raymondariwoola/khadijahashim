About

The story behind the work.

Twenty years ago, I walked into a bank as a young woman balancing her first full-time role with evening degree classes at the University of Wales. I didn't have a roadmap — just drive, curiosity, and a deep belief that people and their experiences are the heartbeat of any great organisation.

Today, as Head of People Experience & Engagement at DIB, I architect the bank's enterprise people strategy — designing programmes that integrate engagement, wellbeing, inclusion, and recognition into one coherent, human-centred experience.

Beyond the professional journey, I am a mother to Hamoodi — a healthy, bright, wonderful boy who came into the world at just 24 weeks and taught our family the true meaning of resilience and hope.
